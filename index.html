MC Daily Quests – How It Works

One epic vanilla Minecraft quest every day. No mods. Pure adventure.

What is this?

  • AI generates a new quest daily at midnight UTC
  • Works in any world seed (Java or Bedrock)
  • 100% vanilla – no mods, no commands
  • Quests focus on exploration, puzzles, light survival

How to Play (3 Steps)

  1. Read today’s quest (title, lore, steps)
  2. Enter your world seed → click “Attach & Share”
  3. Play in your world! Share progress with friends

Voting (1 per day)

  • Click the thumb_up under any quest
  • You get 1 vote per day (resets at midnight)
  • Top-voted quest gets a gold star badge
  • Optional: Check “Quest Complete” to auto-vote

Pro Tips

  • Share your seed link: nagoh.us/MCQUESTS/?seed=12345
  • Check past quests in the Archive (now grid-style!)
  • Leaderboard and stats are coming soon!
